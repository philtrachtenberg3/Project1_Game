How will the next number compare?

How To Play

Purpose: Finish the deck of cards before all piles disappear!

  • The first 9 cards from the deck are placed face-up on the board
  • You must:
    1. Choose a card on the board (ex. A, 9, etc...)
    2. Indicate whether you think the next card will be HIGHER, LOWER, OR EVEN than the card you selected

    If you are correct, the next person goes!

    If you are incorrect, the pile disappears :-(

    Good luck!
